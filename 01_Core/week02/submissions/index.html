My RV History

I am on my way to my 3rd different career in 4.5 years at Red Ventures.

I started as a CE analyst/associate out of school and through a combination of timing and relevant experience, joined the inaugural Analytics team in August of 2017.

After leading data integration/reporting strategy for multiple new partnerships (and refusing to do so again after Amex...), I moved to the central Common Data Model team where I focused on building out standardized reporting and advanced analytics tools using Tableau.
